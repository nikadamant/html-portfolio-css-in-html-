The Best Movies According to Nikita

1. The Shawshank Redemption

Ranked as one of the best movies of all time. A powerful story of hope and friendship.

2. The Godfather

An epic tale of family, loyalty, and power. A timeless classic.

3. Pulp Fiction

Quentin Tarantino's groundbreaking film with sharp dialogue and interconnected stories.

4. Forrest Gump

An inspiring story of love, determination, and an extraordinary life through history.

5. Fight Club

A provocative look at modern consumerism and identity, with a legendary twist.
